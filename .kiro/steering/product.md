# Product Overview

## 中国A股量化交易系统 (A-Share Quantitative Trading System)

A full-stack quantitative trading platform for Chinese A-share market analysis, strategy development, and backtesting.

## Core Features

- **Technical Indicators**: 12 built-in indicators (MA, EMA, MACD, RSI, BOLL, KDJ, CCI, ATR, OBV, WR, DMI, VWAP)
- **Strategy Management**: Multi-indicator strategy configuration with buy/sell conditions
- **Backtesting Engine**: Complete backtesting with Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, win rate calculations
- **Custom Indicators**: User-defined indicator formulas with Python expressions
- **Data Visualization**: K-line charts, indicator overlays, equity curves
- **Data Management**: Automatic A-share data fetching via AkShare, persistent storage
- **Performance Optimization**: Redis caching, database indexing, batch operations

## Target Users

Quantitative traders and analysts working with Chinese A-share market data who need to develop, test, and visualize trading strategies.

## Language

- UI/Documentation: Chinese (Simplified)
- Code: English with Chinese comments where needed
- API responses: Chinese field names and messages
